Who am I?

What Do I Do?

I currently find myself in the post-grad struggle of finding a foothold in a chaotic AI-driven job market. To fill up my time in between applications, I like to practice my skills with various projects. I find value in projects large and small even to just brush up on some basics.

When I'm not working on my technical skills, I practice real-time video streaming and production. I also serve as community lead and handle user management for multiple large online meeting spaces. I will often do case studies on already published videogames as well. These analyze the decisions made in presentation, mechanics, monetization, and other factors to see what works and what doesn't when it comes to development.
